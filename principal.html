From Principal's desk

Aim of education is primarily to inculcate the quality of inquisitiveness which encourages the children to break the mudane and bring the best out of them, thus making them ready to face the challenges of the world.

We, at Burnpur Riverside School, follow the principle "Explore, Exercise and Achieve". Therefore, we provide a platform to every child fro experiential learning, group exploration and building projects with the use of available resources to achieve their individual goals.

We are working diligently to establish a safe, trusting and collaborative culture that is of the utmost importance in achieving student success. Literacy for the 21st Century is our highest priority and together with parents, we trust that each student will be prepared to meet any challenge.

Working with the team of dedicated teachers and staff, who have a big heart for BRS students, I am glad about how we all share a common vision and a strong conviction and belief that every student can evolve to someone significant with strength of character who can contribute effectively to our community and beyond.


Kuljit Kaur
